# Nexora Ventures Admin Portal: User Manual

Welcome to the Nexora Ventures Administration Portal. This manual provides a step-by-step guide on how to manage your platform's content, from global headlines to detailed project listings and blog insights.

---

## 1. Global Site Settings
**Located at:** Dashboard > Settings

This section controls the "High-Impact" text on your homepage. 

| Feature | Description | Impact on Website |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hero Sub-Headline** | The descriptive text appearing directly below your main "Consult, Design, Build..." title. | Sets the tone for visitors. Ideal for a 1-2 sentence mission statement. |
| **Scrolling Ticker** | The moving text inside the blue horizontal bar. | Great for announcements, current focus areas, or core taglines. |

**How to Update:**
1. Navigate to **Settings**.
2. Enter your new text in the respective fields.
3. Click **UPDATE HEADLINE**. Changes are instant.

## 2. Project Asset Management
**Located at:** Dashboard > Projects

Your portfolio is divided into "In Progress" developments and "Already Done" (Completed) projects.

### What you can edit:
- **Title & Description**: The name and core story of the development.
- **Status**: Toggle between *In Progress*, *Already Done*, or *In Future*.
- **Investment Details**: Set the price (e.g., "$50,000") and payment plan (Once vs. Monthly).
- **Time/Duration**: How long the project took or is expected to take.
- **Image**: Upload a high-resolution photo (Max 5MB) or provide a URL.
- **Key Features**: Add unique selling points (e.g., "Smart Grid", "24/7 Security") separated by commas.

**Impact:**
- **In Progress** projects appear in the "Featured Development" section on the Homepage.
- **Already Done** projects appear in the "Our Legacy" section on the Homepage.
- All projects are listed on the main **Projects** page.

## 3. Insights & Blogs
**Located at:** Dashboard > Blogs

Maintain your "Knowledge Hub" by publishing professional articles.

### What you can edit:
- **Content**: Supports full Markdown (titles, bold text, lists).
- **Category**: Tag your posts (Tech, Design, Strategy, Insight).
- **Author**: Name of the writer.
- **Excerpt**: A short 2-line summary for the preview card.
- **Engagement**: The dashboard tracks total "Views" per article.

**Impact:**
- The 3 most recent posts automatically populate the "Latest Insights" section on the Homepage.
- Full articles are accessible via the **Blog** page.

---

## 4. Partner Management
**Located at:** Dashboard > Partners

Manage the brand logos of your collaborators and strategic partners.

**How to Update:**
1. Upload a logo (transparent PNG/SVG recommended).
2. Add the Partner Name.
3. Add optional details.

**Impact:**
- Partners appear in the high-speed scrolling carousel at the bottom of the Homepage.

---

## 5. Contact Leads & Queries
**Located at:** Dashboard > Queries

Every time a potential client fills out the "Contact Us" form, it arrives here.

- **Status Tracking**: New queries are marked in blue; you can track their progress.
- **Quick Actions**:
    - **Reply via Email**: Opens your mail app with the client's email pre-filled.
    - **Reply via WhatsApp**: If a phone number is detected in the message, a "Reply via WhatsApp" button appears for instant mobile communication.

## 6. Best Practices for a Premium Aesthetic

To keep Nexora Ventures looking world-class, follow these guidelines:

1. **Images**: 
   - Use landscape orientation (16:9) for Project and Blog headers.
   - Keep file sizes under 2MB where possible for faster loading.
   - Use high-contrast, professional photography.
2. **Text**:
   - Keep headlines concise.
   - For Blog "Excerpts," stick to 120-150 characters to ensure cards align perfectly.
3. **Logos**:
   - Use white or transparent versions of partner logos for the best look against the dark theme.
